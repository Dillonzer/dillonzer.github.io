How to Use
  • Choose the game you are building for!
  • Click on the chips you'd like to add to your folder!
  • Right click on the Folder chip you'd like to remove from your Folder!
  • Click Export Folder to Export to a human-readable list for sharing!
  • Paste your export in the Import Chip Data box and then click Improt Folder to easily share and edit other's lists!
Searching
  • Type the Chip Name into the Search Bar and click Enter or hit the Filter button
  • Type the Chip Code into the Search Bar to filter via Chips. You can even do comma seperated for multiple. (ie. A,B)
  • To reset filters erase all the data in both the boxes and click Enter or Filter
