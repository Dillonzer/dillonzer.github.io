PTCGBot is a Twitch Bot similar to MTGBot where you can look up the Card Text of specific Cards right in Twitch Chat! If you'd like to have it in your chat, please join my Discord and let me know your Twitch username!

Command NameDescription
!card [optional:setAbbrev] [cardName]Displays the card text
!cardhelpLinks to this website!
!setcodesLinks to this website to show you the set codes!
